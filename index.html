Our Story

Born from a Passion for Beautiful Tables

"We travel the world — from Portugal to Provence, Italy to Istanbul — to find the most extraordinary pieces."

Maison Margaux was founded by a team of event industry insiders who grew tired of settling for uninspiring table settings. Based at Worlds End Studios in the heart of Chelsea, we bring together the finest artisan manufacturers and designers from across the globe.

Every piece tells a story — of the maker, the country, the craft. Whether hosting an intimate dinner for twelve or furnishing an entire hotel, our philosophy never changes: every table deserves to be as beautiful as the food it holds.

Proud members of Walpole — the official body for British luxury — and trusted by some of the world\'s most prestigious venues and brands.

How It Works

From Vision to Tablescape

01
CONSULTATION

We begin with a personal conversation to understand your event, aesthetic vision, guest count and any specific requirements. Our team listens first, then suggests.

02
CURATION

Our design team hand-selects pieces that work together beautifully — cutlery, linens, crockery, glassware — creating a cohesive look tailored entirely to your vision.

03
DELIVERY & SETUP

We deliver to your venue and assist with full tablescape setup if needed. Every piece arrives pristine, packaged and ready to create the most memorable impression.

04
COLLECTION

After your event, we collect everything. You focus entirely on the celebration — we handle all the logistics. A seamless, sustainable experience from start to finish.
